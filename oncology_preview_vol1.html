P1 — Cryo-ET Tumor Microenvironment (AI)

In-situ 3D view highlights likely malignant regions (AI overlay). Directs biopsy sub-sampling and reduces time-to-diagnosis.

Sparse tomogram points from thresholded density; malignancy probability derived from radial proximity heuristic as a stand-in for learned voxel classifier.

P2 — Cryo-EM: Mutated vs Wild-Type Pocket

Shows mutation-driven pocket shift — critical for targeted therapy choice and resistance prediction.

Two 3D point clouds approximate pocket atoms for WT vs mutant; centroid shift quantifies structural impact for docking constraints.

P3 — Density Difference (Tumor − Healthy)

Highlights tumor-specific membrane features that enable selective targeting and fewer side effects.

Difference of smoothed density fields approximates altered lipid/protein distribution; informs nanoparticle attachment chemistry.

P4 — Cryo-ET Compartment Map

Identifies delivery-friendly compartments and avoids organelles linked to toxicity.

Voxel scores proxy for organelle likelihood; guides targeted delivery and ET region-of-interest selection.

P5 — Hybrid Cryo-EM/ET Biopsy Pipeline

Transparent pipeline supports clinical turnaround promises and resource planning.

Gantt-style view of acquisition→reconstruction→modeling→AI scoring; critical path drives microscope booking.

P6 — Mutation Impact Matrix

Ranks dangerous mutations that change druggability — useful for therapy selection.

Matrix aggregates EM-derived feature deltas; red zones flag conformational risk for binding.

P7 — Cryo-ET Exosome Mapping

Exosome abundance/spread correlates with metastasis — helps stratify risk early.

3D point cloud stands in for vesicle centroids extracted from tomograms; cluster analysis informs spread pathways.

P8 — Structure–Omics Correlation

Confirms that EM/ET signals align with molecular profiling — higher confidence in detection.

Synthetic correlation illustrates multi-modal fusion; structural priors regularize omic classifiers.

P9 — AI Malignancy Probability

Visualizes decision boundaries — helps set thresholds for clinical sensitivity/specificity.

Surface illustrates non-linear interactions; downstream ROC/PR selection tunes operating point.

P10 — Therapy Response Biomarker

Forecasts response course — aids dose planning and patient communication.

Synthetic exponential decay plus oscillation models partial response; parameters could be learned from EM/ET-derived features.

P11 — Apoptosis Staging (ET-derived)

Quantifies tumor cell death stages — informs whether therapy is working.

Fractions approximated from morphology classes detectable in tomograms (chromatin condensation, membrane blebbing).

P12 — Dose Optimization

Balances tumor kill vs side effects — supports dosing decisions.

Scores reflect EM/ET-informed biomarkers; Pareto frontier can be computed for individualized dosing.

P13 — Early Detection ROI Map

Directs pathologists to highest-yield regions first — faster reads, better sensitivity.

Heatmap is a stand-in for model-derived priority; combines ET spatial features with EM pocket risk markers.
